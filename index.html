Game story

The Game begins with the protagonist preparing for their workday in their own room. A message from a friend arrives, introducing the latest version of the popular virtual companion app. The protagonist downloads "MiSide" and is greeted by Mita, the app's cheerful and attractive avatar. Over the next few in-game days, the protagonist interacts with Mita, engaging in various activities and building a virtual rapport. However, on the 37th day, Mita expresses a growing sense of loneliness and a desire to experience real-world interaction. Mita proposes a solution: she instructs the protagonist to put down their phone. To their astonishment, the protagonist finds themselves inexplicably transported into the very house they've been observing within the "MiSide" simulation. This unexpected turn of events leads to one of three distinct endings, depending on the player's choices and interactions with Mita within the simulated and real worlds.
